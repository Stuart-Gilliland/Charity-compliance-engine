1. Purpose

This policy explains how long Strathaven Community Trust keeps information and how it is securely disposed of. It supports good governance and compliance with data protection law.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all records held by the charity, whether digital or paper-based.

3. Principles

Data is kept only as long as necessary

Retention meets legal and regulatory requirements

Disposal is secure and appropriate

4. Typical Retention Periods

Trustee records: minimum 6 years after leaving office (OSCR Requirement)

Staff records: in line with employment law

Volunteer records: up to 6 months after role ends

Service Users/Participants: up to 6 months after use ends

Financial records: minimum 6 years (HMRC Requirement)

Safeguarding records: retained as long as necessary and reviewed regularly

5. Secure Disposal

Digital records are securely deleted

Paper records are shredded or securely destroyed

6. Responsibility

The Board oversees compliance. Day-to-day responsibility sits with the Secretary and DPO.

7. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as required.
