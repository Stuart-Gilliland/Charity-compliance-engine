1. Purpose

This guidance sets out acceptable use of Strathaven Community Trust systems and equipment.

2. Scope

Applies to all trustees, staff, and volunteers.

3. General Principles

Charity systems are for charity business only

Users must act responsibly and securely

Personal use is not permitted on charity-owned devices

4. Systems and Accounts

Individual Microsoft 365 accounts must be used

Accounts must not be shared

MFA must remain enabled

5. Devices

Charity devices must not have personal software installed

Devices must be kept secure and locked when unattended

Personal devices may be used securely where approved

6. Email and Internet Use

Charity email accounts must be used for charity business

Care must be taken with links and attachments

7. Data Handling

Personal data must be handled in line with data protection policies

Files should be stored in SharePoint, not locally

8. Breaches

Misuse of systems may result in access being withdrawn.

9. Review

This guidance will be reviewed annually.
