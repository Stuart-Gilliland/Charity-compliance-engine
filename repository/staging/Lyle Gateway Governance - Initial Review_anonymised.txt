Working assumption for this review
For the purpose of this review, the working assumption is that there is no complete, formally
approved, or consistently applied set of policies and procedures currently in place across
the organisation.
While individual documents, draft policies, and informal practices may exist, this framework
treats the organisation as starting from a clean baseline. This approach allows trustees to:
 Take an objective view of what a compliant charity should have in place
 Identify gaps without needing to validate existing documents at this stage
 Prioritise actions based on legal obligation, risk, and good practice
 Avoid assuming compliance where this may not be demonstrable
Any existing documents can be reviewed, refined, and adopted during the implementation
phase where they meet current legal and good practice expectations.
